About project EKL COUPON SYSTEM

Admin

Admin user profile, has privileges of managing the coupon system. The Admin has privileges to create or delete two kinds of user profiles: Company and Customer. The Admin can update the mail and password of a Company or the name and password of a Customer. Admin is forbidden from creating or updating company coupons, nor purchesing coupons as customer. When Admin deletes a Company, all the company details erased from the system, but all of its coupons are deactivated and not deleted. When Admin deletes a Customer, all the customer details erased from the system, including its coupons.

Company

Company user profile, has privileges in the details and coupons areas. A Company is allowed to update its mail and system password, but not the company unique name. A Company can create new coupons, updating coupon details, deactivating coupons and reactivate inactive coupons. Coupon name has to be unique in the coupon system. Company coupons can not be deleted from the data-base of the coupon system and they will be deactivated, in order to ease the reactivation of the coupon when wanted. Company can display its coupons by different views: by top price, coupon end date or by coupon category type.

Customer

Customer user profile, has privileges in the details and coupons areas. A Customer is allowed to update its system password customer name the the is unique in the system. Customer can display coupons all the companies availiable and activate coupons by top price, by coupon catecory type or without filters. Then the Customer can purchse those coupons. Customer can also display its own purchased coupons. Purchasing a coupon is limited to one per customer. Deactivated coupons are not visible to customers to purchase, but available to view if they already purchased them.
